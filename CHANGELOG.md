## 0.1.0

- Initial release.
- AES-256-GCM encryption of `.env` files via the `fl_env` CLI tool.
- Native decryption on Android (API 23+) and iOS (13.0+).
- Typed Dart accessors: `get`, `getRequired`, `getInt`, `getBool`, `getDouble`, `getUri`, `getList`.
- `FlEnvFakeChannel` testing harness for consumer app unit tests.
